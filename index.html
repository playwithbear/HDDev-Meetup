About HDdev

HDdev is a meetup and networking group based in Huddersfield and surrounding areas. Set up due to a lack of local networking opportunities outside of Leeds or Manchester, this group is not technology specific, offering a little something for anyone within the web industry.

If you have something to say, we'd love to have you! Please see our speakers section for more information.
